RESCUED TODAY
IN PAMPANGA ‼️‼️‼️

We received numerous reports and tags on social media yesterday. WE WERE SHOCKED TO SEE THIS DOG WITH A BIG MASS HANGING DOWN HER CHIN! Our hearts melted thinking of the discomfort and possible pain that this dog is suffering from.

We immediately coordinated with one of our volunteers in the area to search for the dog. Our volunteer found out that the dog has an owner, the dog’s name is Cindy. As per owners, Cindy got into a vehicular accident year back, then the lump started to grow. CINDY HAS BEEN CARRYING THIS MASS FOR 2 YEARS ALREADY!

The owners were hesitant during the rescue of Cindy, but the poor dog badly needs help. We agreed that Cindy will be returned but they will have to sign an agreement that they will secure her at all times and will not let her roam in the streets anymore.

Our pets are our responsibility, if we do not provide for their needs, if we do not get treatment when they are sick - that is also considered NEGLECT & CRUELTY! Cindy will undergo series of tests to check her condition, but she will most likely have a surgery to relieve her from the discomfort from this mass in her chin.
